Brief Introduction

I am a Master student in Machenical Engineering at the School of Vehicle and Mobility, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shengbo Eben Li. I received the B.S. degree in Vehicle Engineering at the School of Vehicle and Mobility from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2020.

I am interested in reinforcement learning (RL) combined with optimal control, especially safe RL (where certain state constraints are imposed on the agent), and their applications to autonomous driving.

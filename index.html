Brief Introduction

I am a Master student in Mechanical Engineering at the School of Vehicle and Mobility, Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Shengbo Eben Li. I am also co-advised by Prof. Jianyu Chen, who leads the Intelligent System and Robotics Lab at Institute of Interdisciplinary Information Sciences, Tsinghua University. I received the B.S. degree in Vehicle Engineering at the School of Vehicle and Mobility from Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2020.

I am interested in reinforcement learning (RL) combined with optimal control, especially safe RL (where agents should not violate certain state constraints during and/or after the learning process), and their applications to autonomous driving and robotics.
